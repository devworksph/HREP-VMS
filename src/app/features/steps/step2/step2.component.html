House of Representatives Legislative Information Resources Management Department (LIRMD) DATA PRIVACY NOTICE & CONSENT

The Legislative Information Resources Management Department (LIRMD) of the House of Representatives (HRep) is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR).

PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION

The personal information you provide through this form will be collected and processed to:

  • Facilitate your request, inquiry, or transaction;
  • Provide access to LIRMD programs, services, and facilities;
  • Improve accessibility, inclusivity, and overall service quality;
  • Ensure website functionality, performance, and security; and
  • Comply with government reporting, documentation, and audit requirements

DATA COLLECTED

Depending on the nature of your transaction, we may collect the following

  • Technical Information (automatically collected): IP address, browser type, device information, access date and time, and pages visited

Providing this information is voluntary, but failure to do so may prevent us from processing your request effectively

DATA PROTECTION AND RETENTION

Your personal data will be stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. It will be retained only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law, in accordance with Sections 20–22 of the IRR of RA 10173.

DATA SHARING

Your information will not be shared with unauthorized parties. It may be disclosed only to authorized HRep personnel or other government agencies when required by law or regulation, consistent with Rule IV, Section 18 of the IRR (principles of transparency, legitimate purpose, and proportionality).

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, or withdraw your personal data, and to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) pursuant to Sections 16–19 of the Data Privacy Act and its IRR.
